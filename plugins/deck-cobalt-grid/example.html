WHAT CHANGED
Q3
A short note

We stopped routing every decision through a meeting.

Every previously-weekly sync was replaced by a single channel and an active owner. The owner has the keys. Anyone with a question writes it down. The owner decides — or makes a 60-second video saying why not. No one waits.
